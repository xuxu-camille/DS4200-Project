Introduction

This project explores our single combined dataset, cleaned.csv. By visualizing the data, we can uncover patterns and relationships not obvious from raw numbers alone.

Data Information

Our cleaned.csv file includes columns such as OPE ID, School, State, Zip Code, School Type, Dependent/Independent student counts for multiple quarters, and total quarterly counts.

  • Chart 1 (Bar Chart): Top 10 schools by Quarterly Total_Q1.
  • Chart 2 (Line Chart): Aggregate Quarterly Total_Q1 through Quarterly Total_Q5 across all schools, shown over quarters Q1..Q5.
  • Chart 3 (Scatter Plot): Relationship between Dependent Students_Q1 and Independent Students_Q1 for each school.
  • Chart 4 (Pie/Donut Chart): Breakdown of School Type by sum of Quarterly Total_Q1.
  • Chart 5 (Stacked Bar Chart): Summed Dependent vs. Independent students for each quarter (Q1..Q5).

Conclusion

In conclusion, cleaned.csv offers insight into how different schools and student types vary across quarters. Future exploration might involve deeper analysis of trends by state, or how these totals compare across multiple years.
